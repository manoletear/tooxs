
# Stratwell Consulting Website Clone

## Design System
- **Color palette**: Dark navy primary (#1B2541), white text on dark backgrounds, light gray backgrounds for sections
- **Typography**: Serif font (like Playfair Display) for headings with italic emphasis, clean sans-serif (Inter) for body text
- **Style**: Professional, corporate consulting aesthetic with full-width hero images with dark overlays, glassmorphism navbar, rounded cards
- **Layout**: Full-bleed sections, generous whitespace, 10px rounded corners

## Pages to Build (6 separate routes)

### 1. Home (`/`) — Landing page
- **Hero**: Full-screen background image with dark overlay, large serif heading "Strategy That Powers *Your Next Level* of Growth", subtitle + "Book a Consultation" CTA button with arrow icon
- **Who We Are**: Stats section with animated counters (92% clients, $48M revenue, 200+ businesses, 15+ years)
- **Our Services**: 4 service cards (Business Strategy, Operations Optimization, Digital Transformation, Financial Advisory) with icons, linking to /services. Side CTA card with background image "Start Your Growth Journey"
- **Case Studies**: 3 featured case studies with images, category badges, and descriptions
- **Testimonials**: Horizontal scrolling carousel with client photos, quotes, names, and titles
- **Our Goal**: Split layout — text left, image right, "Transforming Strategy into Results"
- **Our Approach**: 4-step process (Understand, Strategize, Implement, Measure) with numbered icons
- **Trusted By**: Logo marquee/row of 8 client logos
- **CTA Section**: Full-width background image with "Ready to Transform Your Business?" heading and CTA button
- **Footer**: Company info, nav links, social icons

### 2. Services (`/services`)
- Full-screen hero with "Our Expertise" heading
- Detailed service cards for each of the 4 services
- CTA section

### 3. About Us (`/about`)
- Full-screen hero with "About Us" heading
- Company story, mission, values
- Team section
- CTA section

### 4. Case Studies (`/case-studies`)
- Full-screen hero with "Case Studies" heading
- Grid/list of case study cards with images and summaries

### 5. FAQs (`/faqs`)
- Full-screen hero with "Frequently Asked Questions" heading
- Accordion-style FAQ items (10 questions)
- CTA to book consultation

### 6. Contact Us (`/contact`)
- Full-screen background image with dark overlay
- Left side: heading "Let's *Start* the Conversation", description, phone, email, address with icons
- Right side: White form card with fields (Full Name, Email, Phone, Date, Company Name, Service dropdown, Message textarea, Submit button)

## Shared Components
- **Navbar**: Glassmorphism/frosted glass effect, logo left, nav links center (Home, Services, About Us, Case Studies, FAQs), "Contact Us" button right, mobile hamburger menu
- **Footer**: Dark background, company info, quick links, contact details, social media icons
- **Page Hero**: Reusable full-screen hero with background image + dark overlay + centered title (used on Services, About, Case Studies, FAQs)

## Key Interactions
- Smooth scroll animations on section reveal
- Testimonial carousel auto-scroll
- Animated stat counters on scroll into view
- Hover effects on cards and buttons
- Mobile responsive across all pages

## Images
Will use high-quality stock photos from Unsplash for hero backgrounds and section images to match the professional consulting aesthetic.
